HOLLOW KNIGHT

What is Hollow Knight?

Explore the World

The character we play has no name officially, but the community call him 'knight'.

In this game, we will play as a knight who want to do what he should have done. You will explore Hallownest, fight so many enemies, bosses, and get items to end Radiance's tyranny. Because the vast map and someplaces need certain ability to access them, this game make us go to sampe places many times to complete the game.

Hollow Knight is a Metroidvania game published at 2017 by a company called Team Cherry. You can play this game with pc, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, and Play Station 4. Most people give this game a good review, start from the game itself, background music, arts, building, and even the atmosphere.

Gameplay

At the beginning, knight can only do some base movement like jump, walk to the right or left, look down or up, attack, and heal. The more you explore, the more ability you gain. Dash, climb the wall, double jump, and there many more abilities that can be obtained to complete this game.
